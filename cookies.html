Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

At Allisgreen Gardening, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website, improve site functionality, and provide personalized content and features. Cookies help us analyze website traffic, remember your preferences, and ensure that our website runs smoothly.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for our website to function properly. They enable core functionalities such as security, page navigation, and access to secure areas of the site. Without these cookies, some parts of our site may not work correctly.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are the most visited, and any error messages you might encounter. These cookies help us improve the performance and user experience of our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests. They also help limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. We do not use targeted advertising ourselves but may use third-party services that employ these cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

We may employ third-party service providers, such as analytics and advertising partners, who set cookies on our site to help us monitor traffic and website usage, or to display personalized content. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies, block third-party cookies, and notify you when cookies are set. However, disabling cookies may impact your experience on our website and limit certain functionalities.
